Delphis Venture Investment Dashboard

An interactive portfolio dashboard for the Delphis Venture Investment Club — a group of 10–12 members jointly investing through a Vanguard brokerage account.

What This Is

This dashboard gives every club member a clear, up-to-date view of the group's portfolio without needing to log into Vanguard. It tracks all holdings, monthly performance, income, and key events in one shareable page.

What's Inside

The dashboard has six tabs:


Overview — Total portfolio value, YTD gain/loss, and a month-by-month value chart
All Holdings — Full list of every stock, ETF, mutual fund, and CD with current price, shares, market value, cost basis, and unrealized gain/loss. Filterable by asset type.
Winners & Losers — Side-by-side ranking of top performers and biggest losers
Top Positions — Largest holdings by market value
Income — Monthly dividend and interest income breakdown
Events — Notable portfolio events (mergers, splits, new purchases, deposits/withdrawals)
